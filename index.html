What is BIS?

The Business Integration Services team owns integration governance, vendor relationship management, and release coordination across Tyler Technologies' Courts & Justice division. BIS serves as the operational bridge between CMS providers, state court administrations, and Tyler's core product teams.

New to BIS? Start with the Engagement Guide to understand intake processes, SLAs, and how to route your request to the right team.
